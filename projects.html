Let’s Collaborate!

What does a partnership with us look like?

We work on semester-long, pro-bono projects. In the past, we've partnered with 30+ professors, startups, and nonprofits both locally and internationally. Got a project idea but don’t have sufficient time or resources to allocate? We will work with you to assemble the dream team for your project. We are currently open for applications for our fall projects!

What we can do:

Through partnerships, we hope to engage communicative organizations and build a transparent, prolonged relationship with our mentors and team. We utilize design thinking frameworks in every project we take on, in order to create innovative, thoughtful, and intentional solutions that create a tangible impact with the user in mind.

A breakdown of our club:

Our members' skillsets are diverse and include experience with rapid prototyping, web and app development (frontend and backend), user research and interviews, electrical engineering, patent law, 3D modeling, organizational reports, and much more.
